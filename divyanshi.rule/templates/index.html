Key Legal Metrology Requirements for E-commerce

Standard Units (Section 11)

All weights, measures must use metric system: kg, g, mg for weight; m, cm, mm for length; l, ml for volume

Pre-packaged Goods (Section 18)

Must declare net quantity, manufacturer details, and standard quantities on packaging

Pricing Compliance (Section 11)

Price quotes and announcements must only use standard measurement units
